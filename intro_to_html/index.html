What is Circuit Bending?

Circuit bending is taking apart, usually sound making electronics and making internal connecctinos that were not intended by the manufacture to get new sounds. often terryfiying sounds.

It is also possible to circuit bend video processsing electronics like video mixers or digital cameras.

Audio Circuit Bending

Circuit Bending sound making electronics is probably the most common form of circuit bending. It is possible to bend anything from toys for children to vintage drum machine to a birthday card that plays a song when you open it.
