What is Circuit Bending?

Accidentally discovered by artist Reed Ghazala, circuit bending is taking apart electronics and making new, unintended, connections on the PCB. Most of the time circuit bending is done with electronics that make sound, but it can also be done to video processors or digital camera. The intention behind doing it is to bring new life into an otherwise unwanted electronic device.

Circuit Bending Sound

Circuit Bending sound making electronics is probably the most common form of circuit bending. It is possible to bend anything from children's toys to vintage drum machine to a birthday card that plays a song when you open it.
